PROJECTS

Axis Overseas Careers

Axis Overseas Careers is a comprehensive mobile application developed to provide a seamless and efficient platform for students interested in pursuing education abroad. Created in collaboration with the study abroad consultant team, the app offers a range of features designed to streamline the entire study abroad process, from exploration to application and beyond.

SCREENSHOTS

KEY FEATURES

  1. Course and University Search: The app enables students to explore a wide range of courses and universities worldwide, providing detailed information about academic programs, entry requirements, and campus facilities.

  2. Application Guidance: Axis Overseas Careers offers step-by-step guidance and support in the application process. Students can easily access application forms, upload documents, and track the progress of their applications directly through the app.

  3. Document Management: The app allows students to securely store and manage their important documents, such as passports, academic transcripts, and recommendation letters, ensuring easy access when required during the application process.

Axis Language School

Axis Language School is a comprehensive language school app designed to provide an immersive and interactive learning experience for students enrolled through our agency. As the lead developer, I spearheaded the development process and implemented a range of features to enhance the learning journey.

SCREENSHOTS

KEY FEATURES

  1. User Role Differentiation: Axis Language School offers separate login portals for students, tutors, and administrators, ensuring personalized experiences and secure access to relevant features.

  2. Live Classes and Video Lectures: The app provides students with the opportunity to participate in live virtual classes conducted by experienced language tutors. Additionally, a vast library of recorded video lectures is available for students to revisit concepts at their own pace.

  3. Assignments and Study Materials: To reinforce learning, Axis Language School allows tutors to assign interactive assignments and provide study materials within the app. Students can complete assignments and access study resources directly through their individual profiles.

  4. Progress Tracking: The app incorporates a comprehensive progress tracking system, enabling students to monitor their performance and track their learning journey. They can view their scores, completion rates, and areas of improvement, fostering a sense of motivation and accountability.

  5. Attendance Monitoring: Tutors and administrators have the ability to mark and monitor student attendance through the app. This feature facilitates seamless tracking of student participation and enables timely intervention when necessary.

  6. Communication and Support: Axis Language School includes built-in messaging functionality that allows students, tutors, and administrators to communicate directly within the app. This facilitates effective communication, clarifies doubts, and ensures a smooth flow of information.
